Philhis Augmented Reality

Learn using Philhis

Explore Philippine history through an interactive e-learning application with augmented reality. Immerse yourself in significant events that shaped the country's past, and gain a deeper understanding of the lessons and values they impart. Access the app on your mobile device and scan the markers to view historical events. With this innovative approach to learning, you can experience Philippine history like never before.

Explore, Interact, and Learn with 3D Models

Explore significant events in our country's past through immersive digital models that bring the stories to life. See the heroes, landmarks, and artifacts in stunning 3D detail, and interact with them in ways that are not possible with traditional textbooks or classroom lectures. Download our app and access our library of markers to unlock a wealth of information and insights into the events that shaped our nation. Whether you are a student, a history buff, or just curious about the Philippines' rich cultural heritage, our AR app is the perfect tool to engage and inspire you.

PhilHis Quiz

Test your knowledge of Philippine history with our interactive Quiz Game feature! Challenge yourself and others in our online quizzes with time-based questions, and see how you rank on our leaderboard. Teachers can create and administer engaging quizzes for their students in an easy and interactive way. With our app, teachers can take their classroom to the next level and make learning fun and interactive for their students.
